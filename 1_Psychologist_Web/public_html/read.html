··Someone asked: "What is the impact of history on people?"

··The answer is: "It can make people more calm and calmer."

The biggest benefit of reading history is that to broaden the horizons of people. When the horizon is broad, the whole person will become suddenly clear and think northing is not big deal.

When you see the cruelty of war, the passing of the good scene, and the joys and sorrows of the people, and then look back at your present troubles, you will feel so insignificant.

History is very long, even the life we feel is endless, and it is only a drop in the ocean on the long river of history, not to mention those little things that are unpleasant.

Don't be too persistent about everything. For those things that have no benefit or influence on ourselves, we must selectively avoid them and not waste our feelings on them.
