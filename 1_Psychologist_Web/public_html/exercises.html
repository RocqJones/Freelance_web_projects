Exercises
“Doing sports will make you happy.”

—— This sentence is based on scientific theory.

During exercise, the brain secretes an endorphin substance that helps people synthesize serum and dopamine in the body. And dopamine is a chemical that makes people feel happy. In the process of exercise, all our attention is focused on the movement, and the nerves that have been tight will be relaxed. The mood will naturally become better Therefore, exercise is a very good choice when there are some negative emotions in your heart that make it difficult to vent.

For Example

Hong Kong actress, Sammi Cheng, had suffered from a very serious depression. During that period, she overheated and caused his body to be completely out of shape.

In order to make her body and mentality healthy, she started running eight kilometers per day amd persisted for more than ten years. She once mentioned in Weibo that running and sweating can excrete waste from the body, mind, and soul.

After many years of sports, Zheng Xiuwen, who has been nearly 50 years old, is still known as the "never aging goddess" of the entertainment industry. There is no such thing as a bad mood that can't be solved by running for an hour. If not, run for another hour tomorrow.
